Large language models are reshaping how organizations build, deliver, and consume analytics. This book is the practical guide to what's real, what's hype, and what practitioners actually need to know to put AI to work in their BI stack.

About Ryan

That path gave me something unusual: I know what buyers actually need because I was one, I know how vendors actually build and sell because I did it, and I'm deep in the AI transformation that's reshaping both sides. When I advise a vendor on their analyst submission, I've written one. When I help a company evaluate tools, I've built the tools they're evaluating.
